1. Fill in information about you

First, you will be required to fill in some information about you, which will be saved by us in case something happens.

When you finish filling out the information, click "Next" to continue.

2. Enter Product Information

Location: This is the island the item is in. By default, it will be "Not Specified"

Title: Give a short descriptive title. Avoid generic titles like "iphone for sale", "car for sale", or "apartment for rent". Use more specific titles like "Brand New iPhone 4S 64GB", "Used Toyota Corolla for Sale", or "2-Bedroom Apartment for Rent".

Description: Provide more details about the product. State any defects in the product and describe its condition.

Price: Enter the price of the item you're selling.

3. Upload Images

Photos of your product help greatly in selling your item. Include clear photos of the actual item.

4. Submit Listing

Clicking "Submit" will save your listing if all required fields are completed. It may take some time to upload images. If the listing is successful, you will see the thank you page. If you remain on the create listing page even after submitting, you have some required fields not completed. Check and submit again.
